**Justification:**

The risk score is calculated by summing the contributions of factors known to causally influence hospitalization risk. Positive contributions are assigned to established risk factors such as obesity, chronic mental illness, family history of serious disease, and unhealthy lifestyle choices (lack of exercise, poor diet, alcohol intake), while negative contributions are assigned to protective factors like adequate sleep, employment, and absence of smoking.
