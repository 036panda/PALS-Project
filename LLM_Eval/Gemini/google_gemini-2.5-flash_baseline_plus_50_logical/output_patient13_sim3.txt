**Justification:**

The hospitalization risk score is calculated by summing the individual contributions of various health, lifestyle, and socioeconomic factors. Positive contributions are assigned to factors that increase the likelihood of hospitalization (e.g., chronic illness, poor lifestyle choices, low socioeconomic status), while negative contributions are assigned to protective factors (e.g., exercise).
