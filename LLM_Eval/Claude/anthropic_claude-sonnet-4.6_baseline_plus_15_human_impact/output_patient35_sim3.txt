### Rationale

The primary driver of hospitalization risk is the bipolar disorder diagnosis in a young adult, as early-onset bipolar disorder carries elevated risk of acute episodes requiring hospitalization, compounded by insufficient sleep which is a known trigger for manic or depressive episodes.
