The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health and lifestyle factors, with particular emphasis on the presence of bipolar disorder, family history of diabetes, and socioeconomic status, which are known to increase the risk of hospitalization due to their potential to exacerbate health conditions. The score reflects a balance between these risk factors and protective factors such as adequate sleep and moderate alcohol intake.
