The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health factors, lifestyle, and demographic characteristics, with a focus on factors that have a direct causal relationship to increased risk of hospitalization, such as age, BMI, family health history, smoking, and current diagnosis. The score reflects the cumulative effect of these factors, adjusted for mitigating factors like regular exercise and adequate sleep.
