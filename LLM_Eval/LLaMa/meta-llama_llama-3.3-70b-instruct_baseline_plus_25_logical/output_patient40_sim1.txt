The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health status, family medical history, and lifestyle factors, with particular emphasis on the bipolar diagnosis, family history of diabetes, and socioeconomic status, which are known to increase the risk of hospitalization. The score reflects the cumulative effect of these factors, adjusted for the presence of some protective factors such as employment and adequate sleep.
