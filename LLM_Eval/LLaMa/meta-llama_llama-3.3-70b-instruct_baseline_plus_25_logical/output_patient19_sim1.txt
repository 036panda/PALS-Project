3. The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the presence of a current diagnosis of depression, socioeconomic status, and other factors that may influence mental and physical health, with a logical and causal basis in the relationships between these factors and the likelihood of hospitalization. The score reflects a moderate risk due to the current diagnosis and socioeconomic status, mitigated by factors such as employment and normal BMI.
