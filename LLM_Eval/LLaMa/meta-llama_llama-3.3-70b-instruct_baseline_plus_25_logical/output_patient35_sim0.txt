The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health factors, lifestyle, and socioeconomic status, with a focus on the causal relationships between these factors and the likelihood of hospitalization. The score reflects the increased risk associated with the individual's overweight status, family history of hypertension, and bipolar diagnosis, balanced by the lower risk due to their younger age, high socioeconomic status, and lack of depression.
