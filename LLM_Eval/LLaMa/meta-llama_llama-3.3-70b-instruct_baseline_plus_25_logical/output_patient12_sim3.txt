The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health factors, lifestyle choices, and demographic characteristics, with a focus on established causal relationships between these factors and increased risk of hospitalization. The score reflects the cumulative impact of the individual's age, BMI, family health history, smoking status, and other relevant factors on their overall hospitalization risk.
