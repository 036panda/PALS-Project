The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health factors, such as BMI, family health history, and current diagnosis, which have a direct causal relationship with health risks, and lifestyle factors like sleep hours, alcohol intake, and socioeconomic status, which can influence overall health and well-being.
