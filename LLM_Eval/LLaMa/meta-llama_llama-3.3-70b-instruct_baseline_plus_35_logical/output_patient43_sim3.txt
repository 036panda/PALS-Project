The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the summation of positive and negative contributions from various factors, reflecting their causal relationships with health risks, considering both direct and indirect impacts on an individual's likelihood of hospitalization. This approach integrates a wide range of factors, from medical history and current health status to lifestyle and socioeconomic factors, to provide a comprehensive risk assessment.
