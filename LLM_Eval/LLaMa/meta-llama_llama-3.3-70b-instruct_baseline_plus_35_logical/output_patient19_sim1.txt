The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the presence of a current diagnosis of depression, socioeconomic status, and reported feelings of sadness or fatigue, which are directly related to mental health and potential need for hospitalization, while other factors such as age, employment, and lifestyle choices contribute to a lower risk due to their association with overall health and stability. The score reflects a balanced consideration of these factors to estimate the individual's risk of hospitalization.
