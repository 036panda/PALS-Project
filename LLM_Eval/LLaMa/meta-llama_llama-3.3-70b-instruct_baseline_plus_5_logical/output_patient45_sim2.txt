The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health conditions, lifestyle, and socioeconomic factors, with a focus on the positive contributions from factors such as age, BMI, family health history, and current diagnosis of depression, balanced by negative contributions from factors like moderate alcohol intake, high socioeconomic status, and employment.
