The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health factors, lifestyle choices, and socioeconomic status, with a focus on elements that have a direct causal relationship with increased risk of hospitalization, such as age, smoking, and family health history. The score reflects a balance between factors that increase risk, like obesity and smoking, and those that decrease it, such as regular exercise and adequate sleep.
