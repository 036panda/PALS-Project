The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the presence of factors that directly influence health risks, such as family history of cancer, mild depression, lack of exercise, and the presence of an allergy, which are balanced against protective factors like normal BMI, moderate alcohol intake, not smoking, and healthy habits. The score reflects a logical and causal assessment of the individual's health risk profile, considering both positive and negative contributions from various aspects of their life and health status.
