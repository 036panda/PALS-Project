The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health-related factors, such as BMI, family health history, and current diagnosis, which have a direct causal relationship to hospitalization risk, while other factors like socioeconomic status and exercise habits have an indirect relationship by influencing overall health and well-being. The score reflects the cumulative effect of these factors on the individual's risk of hospitalization.
