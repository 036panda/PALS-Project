The risk score calculation is based on identifying and quantifying the positive and negative contributions of various factors to the individual's health risk profile, considering both direct health indicators and lifestyle factors that could influence health outcomes. The score reflects a balance between factors indicating increased risk, such as family health history and lack of exercise, and those suggesting lower risk, like low alcohol intake and stable employment.
