The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health factors, behaviors, and socioeconomic status, with significant contributions from severe depression, smoking, and low socioeconomic status, which are known to increase the risk of hospitalization due to their impact on both physical and mental health. The score reflects a logical and causal relationship between these factors and the likelihood of hospitalization.
