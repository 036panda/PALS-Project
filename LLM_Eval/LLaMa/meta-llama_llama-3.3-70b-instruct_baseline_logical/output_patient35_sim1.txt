The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health factors, lifestyle, and socioeconomic status, with a focus on established causal relationships between these factors and hospitalization risk. The score reflects the cumulative effect of these factors, with higher contributions from conditions like bipolar diagnosis and family history of hypertension, and lower contributions from factors like employment and high socioeconomic status.
