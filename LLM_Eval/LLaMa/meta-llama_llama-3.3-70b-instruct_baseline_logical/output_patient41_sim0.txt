The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's specific health and demographic factors, with a focus on those that have a direct causal relationship with increased health risks, such as family history of cancer and mild depression. The score reflects the cumulative effect of these factors, with a lower score indicating a lower risk of hospitalization.
