The hospitalization risk score is calculated based on the individual's health indicators, family history, and lifestyle factors that have a direct causal relationship with increased risk of hospitalization, such as higher BMI, family history of diabetes, and insufficient sleep, balanced against protective factors like moderate alcohol intake and non-smoking status. The score reflects a logical and evidence-based assessment of the individual's risk profile.
